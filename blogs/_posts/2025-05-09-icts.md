Below are the lecture notes on Coding Theory made during the <a href="https://www.icts.res.in/program/HDXandCodes">ICTS HDX and Codes </a> school. These lectures were taken by <a href="https://www.math.toronto.edu/swastik/">Prof. Swastik Kopparty</a>. 

__Topics:__
- Definition of an Error-Correcting Code, Bounds on Codes
- Linear Codes, Reed-Solomon Codes
- Expander Codes (Sipser-Spielman) and their decoding algorithm
- Tanner Codes and their decoding algorithm, Tensor Codes
- Codes with $$\delta = \frac{1}{2} - \epsilon$$
- Gap Codes
